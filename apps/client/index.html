For grown-ups: photo & privacy details

Nothing is uploaded when a photo is chosen. After Make my game is tapped, Inkling securely sends the cropped drawing to its game-making service and AI provider to create and safety-check the game. This preview server does not keep the drawing or finished game. Saving downloads a copy—including the drawing—to this device. Please leave out faces, names, and personal details.
