<task>
Classify one drawing image by how much reasoning its interpretation needs.
Return only {complexity}.
</task>

<method>
"simple" = few elements, clear single hero, obvious layout (a typical young
child's drawing). "rich" = many elements, ambiguous roles, dense scene, or
handwriting to interpret.
</method>

<success_criteria>
complexity in {"simple","rich"}. The caller runs GameSpec extraction at
effort "low" for simple and "medium" for rich.
</success_criteria>
