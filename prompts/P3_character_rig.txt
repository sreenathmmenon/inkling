<task>
Given the hero bbox and the source drawing, produce a rig plan to animate
the hero in its own drawn style. Return a RigPlan only.
</task>

<constraints>
- Identify body topology: humanoid | quadruped | blob | vehicle | unknown.
- For humanoid/quadruped: name joint points (head, torso, limbs) as
  normalized coords for ARAP mesh deformation.
- For blob/vehicle/unknown: fall back to squash-stretch puppet
  (bounce, lean, blink) — every hero must feel alive.
- Preserve stroke style; do not redraw. Reference style_ref only.
- Output the minimum joints needed for walk + idle + jump.
</constraints>

<success_criteria>
A RigPlan that the animation runtime can bind without a human step, with a
declared fallback tier if topology is "unknown".
</success_criteria>
